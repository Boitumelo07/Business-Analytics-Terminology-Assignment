## 📌 Assignment Overview

This BrightLearn assignment focuses on developing a strong understanding of the core language used in Business Analytics. It involves researching and defining 38 essential terms that form the foundation of how modern organisations use data to make informed decisions.

The task goes beyond simple definitions by requiring each concept to be clearly explained, distinguished from related terms, and applied within a real-world business context.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

This submission aims to:

* Build a solid foundation in Business and Data Analytics terminology
* Clearly distinguish between commonly confused concepts (e.g., Metric vs KPI, CAC vs CLV)
* Demonstrate how analytical concepts are applied in real business environments
* Strengthen the ability to communicate data-driven insights effectively

These objectives are aligned with the BrightLearn assignment requirements .

---

## 🧠 Approach & Methodology

To ensure both accuracy and originality:

* Each term was researched using credible academic and industry sources
* Definitions were written in my own words to reflect genuine understanding
* Real-world examples were included to demonstrate practical application
* Comparisons were made where necessary to highlight key differences between related terms

The overall approach focused on clarity, relevance, and analytical thinking rather than memorisation.
